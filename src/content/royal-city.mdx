# The Royal City

The Royal City is the central hub of Returners Online where players can engage in social, economic and political activities. It holds the summoning circle and returners gate. The city is split into different districts which have different functions:

![Royal City](/assets/city-center.png)

- **Market District:** This is where players can buy and sell items, access the auction house, and interact with NPC merchants. There are also black markets located inside summoned worlds underworld.
- **Entertainment District:** This area has player owned clubs where players can dance, socialize and drink.
- **Crafting District:** This is where players can access crafting stations and work on their crafting skills.
- **Castle District:** This is where the King and 12 nobles reside and where players can access the returners gate to venture into the world. It also holds the marriage hall where players can get married.
- **Housing District:** This is where players can purchase instance plots to build their own houses and decorate them with furniture and decorations. Plots also come with extra land for farming and raising animals.
