# The Royal City

The Royal City is the central hub of Returners Online where players can engage in social, economic and political activities. It holds the summoning circle and returners gate. The city is split into different districts which have different functions:

![Royal City](/assets/city-center.png)

## Center District

- **Castle District:** This is the center of the Royal City. It also holds the returners gate and the marriage hall where players can get married.

## Ward Districts

These are districts located in each of the 4 different wards in the royal city.

- **Market District:** This district holds the global market where players can buy and sell items.
- **Entertainment District:** This area has player owned clubs where players can dance, socialize and drink.
- **Crafting District:** This is where players can access crafting stations and work on their crafting skills. Crafting stations are owned by players through rental auctions.
- **Housing District:** This is where players can purchase instance plots to build their own houses and decorate them with furniture and decorations. Plots also come with extra land for farming and raising animals.
- **Summoning District:** This is an artificial mana zone in the royal city where players can access the summoning circle to embark to summoned worlds.
- **Battle Arena District:** This is an artificial mana zone in the royal city where players can queue and enter the battle arena for ranked and unranked 5v5 PvP combat.
- **Alter District:** This is where players can access the alter and spend favor points to remove curses.
