# Roads

Roads are trial pathways that lead to either the **Overworld God** or the **Underworld Abyssal God** of a summoned world. Roads that lead to the Overworld God are called **Roads of Ascension**. Roads that lead to the Underworld Abyssal God are called **Roads of Descent**.

![Roads](/assets/roads.png)

Each road is made up of a series of trials. Players must defeat the boss of each trial before they can continue to the next stage. The final trial leads to the ruling deity of that side of the world.

## Road Structure

### Roads of Ascension

Roads of Ascension belong to the **Overworld** and lead heroes toward the god of that summoned world.

| Stage        | Boss     |
| ------------ | -------- |
| First Trial  | Guardian |
| Second Trial | Sentinel |
| Third Trial  | Demigod  |
| Final Trial  | God      |

### Roads of Descent

Roads of Descent belong to the **Underworld** and lead villains toward the Abyssal God of that summoned world.

| Stage        | Boss        |
| ------------ | ----------- |
| First Trial  | Demon       |
| Second Trial | Archdemon   |
| Third Trial  | Demon Lord  |
| Final Trial  | Abyssal God |

## Purpose

Roads are one of the main ways players challenge the strongest beings in summoned worlds. A world hero storyline is completed by progressing through the Roads of Descent and defeating the Abyssal God, while a world villain storyline is completed by progressing through the Roads of Ascension and defeating the Overworld God.
