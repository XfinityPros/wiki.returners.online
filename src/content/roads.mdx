# Roads

Roads are solo trial pathways that lead to either the **Overworld God** or the **Underworld Abyssal God** of a summoned world. Roads that lead to the Overworld God are called **Roads of Ascension**. Roads that lead to the Underworld Abyssal God are called **Roads of Descent**.

![Roads](/assets/roads.png)

All roads are made up of a series of trials. Players must defeat the boss of each trial before they can continue to the next stage. The final trial leads to the ruling deity of that side of the world. There are 5 trials in total for each road which are unlocked sequentially and a completed trial can be repeated or skipped after completion. The final trial can only be attempted by 1 player at a time with a 1 minute cooldown between attempts while allowing only 1 attempt per player with a 2 hour attempt cooldown. While players are in queue they can spectate the current player who is attempting the trial.

Roads contain 6 floors. Floors 1 - 4 are for the first to fourth trial. The 5th floor is a lobby where players can wait for their turn to attempt the final trial. The 6th floor is the final trial itself. Players can only access the 6th floor if they are currently attempting the final trial or if they are spectating the player who is attempting the final trial.

## Road Structure

### Roads of Ascension

Roads of Ascension are located in the **Overworld** and lead villains toward the god of that summoned world.

| Stage        | Boss     |
| ------------ | -------- |
| First Trial  | Warden   |
| Second Trial | Guardian |
| Third Trial  | Sentinel |
| Fourth Trial | Demigod  |
| Final Trial  | God      |

### Roads of Descent

Roads of Descent are located in the **Underworld** and lead heroes toward the Abyssal God of that summoned world.

| Stage        | Boss        |
| ------------ | ----------- |
| First Trial  | Crawler     |
| Second Trial | Demon       |
| Third Trial  | Archdemon   |
| Fourth Trial | Demon Lord  |
| Final Trial  | Abyssal God |
