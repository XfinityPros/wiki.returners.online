# Gods

Gods and Abyssal Gods in Returners Online are powerful world rulers that players can interact with, receive blessings or curses from, follow through storylines, and even challenge to duels.

![Gods in Returners Online](/assets/gods.png)

Each summoned world has two ruling powers:

| Layer      | Ruler       | Player path |
| ---------- | ----------- | ----------- |
| Overworld  | God         | Hero        |
| Underworld | Abyssal God | Villain     |

Each god has their own domain, attribute (main power), and race of followers. At the summoning circle in the royal city players can choose whether to follow the hero path and do quests for the god, or follow the villain path and do quests for the abyssal god. Players can also choose to ignore both paths and just explore the world, but they will miss out on many rewards and story content.

Players can choose to follow a specific god questline (hero/villain) and receive blessings that provide various benefits, but they can also receive curses if they displease the god or lose a duel against them. (Curses are permanent and can only be removed by spending favor at an altar)

Slaying gods grants players a massive wisdom reward towards the “god slayer” title which is the highest title in the game which can be mastered (levelled up) by slaying more gods and receiving more wisdom.
