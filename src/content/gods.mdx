# Gods

Gods in Returners Online are powerful beings that players can interact with, receive blessings or curses from, and even challenge to duels.

![Gods in Returners Online](/assets/gods.png)

Each god has their own domain, attribute (main power), and race of followers. Players can choose to follow a specific god and receive blessings that provide various benefits, but they can also receive curses if they displease the god or lose a duel against them. (Curses are permanent and can only be removed by spending favor at an altar)

Slaying gods grants players a massive wisdom reward towards the "god slayer" title which is the highest title in the game which can be mastered (levelled up) by slaying more gods and receiving more wisdom.
