# Spawns

Spawns are creatures created from a summoned world’s excess mana. Some form naturally within the realm, while others are created by gods. They become hostile when players get too close and can be fought for loot and wisdom.

![Spawns](/assets/spawn-camps.png)

## Spawn Camps

Spawns are usually found in spawn camps scattered across summoned worlds. These camps come in different types and rarities, with stronger camps containing more dangerous enemies and better rewards.

Some spawn camps are tied to specific biomes or god domains. For example, frost spawns may appear more often in snowy regions, while swamp spawns may be common in Aqua’s water domain.

## Camp Objectives

Different spawn camps can have different objectives. Some camps protect loot chests, while others may hold captured guardians or captured demons lords. Heroes can attack demon spawn camps in the overworld, while villains can attack holy spawn camps in the underworld.

## Natural Spawns

Natural spawns are creatures that form naturally from the excess mana in a summoned world. In the overworld they are called creeps while in the underworld they are called skeletons

## God Created Spawns

God created spawns are creatures that are forged by gods by harnessing the excess mana in a summoned world. Unlike natural spawns, god created spawns are more powerful and have unique abilities. They are also created in a linear hiarachy of power in comparison to the power of the god that created them:

| Overworld | Underworld   |
| --------- | ------------ |
| Gods      | Abyssal Gods |
| Demigods  | Demon Lords  |
| Sentinels | Archdemons   |
| Guardians | Demons       |
