# Summoning

Summoning in Returners Online is the process by which players are called upon by a god or goddess to enter different fantasy worlds and undertake various quests and challenges.

After getting summoned players will spawn in a summoning circle in the fantasy world and pick whether to continue as a hero or villain. This choice will determine the class relics the player can use and the quests you can do in the world. Some summoned worlds may let you take on different forms such as an elf and unlock special movement abilities like flying.
