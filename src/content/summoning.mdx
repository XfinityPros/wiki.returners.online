# Summoning

Summoning is a process used by players to enter summoned worlds through gods who bless or curse their relics with special abilities of their choice.

![Summoning Circle](/assets/summoning-circle.png)

{/* Summoning Circles Work like elevators */}

To begin summoning, players must visit the **Summoning Circle** in the Royal City and ready up for teleportation to their selected summoned world. The summoning district is an artificial mana zone and players must have their hero or abyssal hero relic equipped inorder to enter the summoning circle. Each summoned world has its own wisdom requirement so players must farm wisdom by doing activities in other world they can access.

Summoning circles are activitated when at least one player stands within the circle. When that happens the circle will start a 30 second countdown during which other players can join the circle. Once the countdown ends the summoning circle puts up a barrier and all players in the circle will be teleported to the selected summoned world. After arriving players will first have to select blessings to apply to their relic which will work as special abilities.

**NB: Players should not equip relics outside mana zones otherwise they will lose health rapidly as hero and abyssal hero bodies require mana to survive**

## Summoning Process

| Step                          | Description                                                                                                     |
| ----------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **1. Obtain Relic**           | The player buys or crafts a hero or abyssal hero relic from the royal city.                                     |
| **2. Enter summoning circle** | The player visits the Summoning Circle in the Royal City summoning district.                                    |
| **3. Select a World**         | The player selects a summoned world to embark to. Each world has its own wisdom requirement.                    |
| **4. Teleportation**          | After the countdown ends, all players in the circle are teleported to the selected summoned world.              |
| **5. Choose Abilities**       | The player selects abilities to apply to their relic. These will act as special attacks, defenses, and debuffs. |
| **6. Choose a Spawn Region**  | The player selects an unlocked spawn region that has a summoning circle.                                        |

## Spawn Regions

When entering a summoned world for the first time, only the sanctuary region is available. This region is the rear end of the world.

To unlock more spawn regions, players must travel to those regions' summoning circle at least once. If a region has a summoning circle, it can then be selected as a future spawn point.
