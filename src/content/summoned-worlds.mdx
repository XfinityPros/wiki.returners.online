# Summoned Worlds

![Summonded Worlds](/assets/worlds.png)

Summoned Worlds in Returners Online are realms where players are summoned and can complete the story as either a hero or a villain. Upon completion players recieve massive rewards. Players can also embark to these realms to collect rare resources and bring them back to the royal city for crafting or selling. Alternatively players can bring crafted items from the royal city to summoned worlds which is forbidden and requires and certain amount of corruption points to do so. Such items are sold at black markets located inside summoned worlds.

Completing the story as a hero grants players favor points while completing the story as a villain grants players corruption points.

They are characterized by biome, god domain, demon king (underworld) and race.

Currently there are 4 planned summoned worlds:

- Frostveil: Featuring Eira's domain, snow biome and race of dwarves.
- Embagard: Featuring Sung's domain, desert biome and race of lizardfolk.
- Elviandore: Featuring Flora's domain, forest biome and race of elves.
- Skyreach Isles: Featuring Aqua's domain, sky swamp biome and race of harpies. (Most likely to be a paid world while allowing flying with players equipping wings)

Each of the worlds has an underworld which is the demon territory accessed via hell gates.
