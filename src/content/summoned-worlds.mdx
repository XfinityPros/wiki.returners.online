# Summoned Worlds

Summoned Worlds in Returners Online are realms where players are summoned and can complete the story as either a hero or a villain. Upon completion players recieve massive rewards.

![Summoned Worlds](/assets/worlds.png)

Players can also embark to these realms to collect rare resources and bring them back to the royal city for crafting or selling. Alternatively players can bring crafted items from the royal city to summoned worlds which is forbidden and requires and certain amount of corruption points to do so. Such items are sold at black markets located inside summoned worlds.

Completing the story as a hero grants players favor points while completing the story as a villain grants players corruption points.

## World Structure

Every summoned world has two connected layers:

| Layer          | Description                                                                            |
| -------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Overworld**  | The main version of the world, ruled by a god and usually friendly to heroes.          |
| **Underworld** | A darker mirror of the same world, usually friendly to villains and hostile to heroes. |

The Underworld shares the same base world, but changes the mood, enemies, resources, objectives, and danger level.

Players can move between the Overworld and Underworld through **Hell Gates**.
