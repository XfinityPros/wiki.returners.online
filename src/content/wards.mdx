# Royal City Wards

The royal city is divided into 4 wards: Alpha, Beta, Zeta and Omega.

## Ward Warfare

Ward Warfare involves players venturing into summoned worlds and capturing structures in the overworld as well as defending their captured structures from other wards.

When players are equipped with their ward crest capes they can engage in Ward Warfare. Ward war is mainly capturing watch towers and outposts inside summoned worlds. Players are awarded ward points when they take part in ward warfare.
